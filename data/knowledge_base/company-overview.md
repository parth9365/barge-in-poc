# NovaTech Solutions - Company Overview

## About NovaTech

NovaTech Solutions is a B2B SaaS company founded in 2019 by Maria Chen and David Park in Austin, Texas. The company specializes in AI-powered project management tools designed for mid-size to enterprise software teams. NovaTech's flagship product, NovaBoard, serves over 12,000 companies worldwide and manages more than 2 million active projects.

NovaTech's mission is to eliminate project management overhead so teams can focus on building great software. The company has raised $85 million in funding across Series A through C rounds, with the most recent Series C led by Horizon Ventures in March 2025.

## Leadership Team

- **Maria Chen, CEO & Co-Founder**: Former engineering director at Atlassian. Led the development of NovaBoard's AI engine. Named to Forbes 30 Under 30 in 2021.
- **David Park, CTO & Co-Founder**: PhD in Machine Learning from Stanford. Previously a senior researcher at Google DeepMind. Holds 8 patents in natural language processing.

## Company Culture

NovaTech operates on four core values:

1. **Ship Fast, Learn Faster**: Two-week sprint cycles with continuous deployment. Every engineer deploys to production on their first week.
2. **Data Over Opinions**: All product decisions are backed by usage analytics and A/B testing.
3. **Radical Transparency**: All-hands meetings every two weeks. Company metrics dashboard is visible to everyone.
4. **Customer Obsession**: Every team member spends one day per quarter doing customer support.
